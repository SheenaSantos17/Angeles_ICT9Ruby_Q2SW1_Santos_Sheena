MY FAVORITE MUSIC ARTISTS

Get to know me more through my favorite musics artists. Both locally and internationally, they continue to prove that music trancends language.


BINI

Cherry On Top

"Cherry on Top" is a song by the Filipino girl group Bini. It was released as a digital single on July 11, 2024, through Star Music. "Cherry on Top" is a bubblegum pop song produced by Shintaro Yasuda and Skylar Mones, along with compositions by Boy Matthews, Gaby Ramirez, Shintaro Yasuda and Skylar Mones

I Feel Good

"I Feel Good" is the Pre-Album single of Filipino girl group BINI, and their third single released in 2022. It was released on September 22, 2022 at 6:00 pm PHT on all digital streaming platforms. The song was also part of BINI's sophomore album Feel Good as its third track.

Karera

"Karera" is the Pre-Album single of Filipino girl group BINI, and their first single released by Star Music in 2023. It was released on September 22, 2023 at 12:00 mn PHT on all digital streaming platforms. The song was also part of BINI's first EP Taalarawan as its first track.

Patropiko

"Pantropiko" is the Pre-Album single of Filipino girl group BINI, and their second single released in 2023. It was released on November 17, 2023 at 12:00 am PHT on all digital streaming platforms. The song is also part of BINI's first Extended Play Talaarawan as its third track.



ENHYPEN

Dark Blood

Dark Blood is the fourth extended play (EP) by South Korean boy group Enhypen. It was released on May 22, 2023, through Belift Lab. The album consists of six tracks, including the lead single "Bite Me".

Orange Blood

Orange Blood is the fifth extended play by South Korean boy band Enhypen. It was released on November 17, 2023, through Belift Lab, alongside its title track, "Sweet Venom".

Romance: Untold

Romance: Untold is the second Korean-language studio album by South Korean boy band Enhypen, released on July 12, 2024, through Belift Lab. The album consists of nine tracks, including the lead single "XO". It reached number one in South Korea, Japan and France, and number two on the US Billboard 200.

Romance: Untold Daydream

Romance : Untold -Daydream- (stylized as ROMANCE : UNTOLD -daydream-) is a repackage of ENHYPEN's second full-length album Romance : Untold. It was released on November 11, 2024 with "No Doubt" serving as the album's title track.



TAYLOR SWIFT

Fearless

Fearless is the second studio album by the American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift. She wrote the majority of the album while touring in 2007–2008 and produced it with Nathan Chapman.

Lover

"Lover" is a song written and recorded by the American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift and the title track of her seventh studio album (2019). Swift conceived it as a timeless love song that could be played at a wedding reception; the lyrics are about an intimate and committed relationship, and the bridge draws on the bridal rhyme "Something old".

Reputation

Reputation is the sixth studio album by the American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift. It was released on November 10, 2017, as her last album with Big Machine Records.

Speak Now

Speak Now is the third studio album by the American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift, released on October 25, 2010, by Big Machine Records. Swift wrote the album entirely herself while touring in 2009–2010 to reflect on her transition from adolescence to adulthood.
